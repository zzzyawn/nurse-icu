üö® Essential Certifications

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Provider: American Heart Association (AHA)

Duration: 2 years

Requirements: Mandatory for most nursing positions

Cost: $45-$75

Format: Blended learning (online + hands-on) or instructor-led

  • Adult, child, and infant CPR
  • AED use and safety
  • Choking relief techniques
  • Team-based resuscitation

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS)

Provider: AHA or AAOS

Duration: 2 years

Prerequisites: Current BLS certification

Cost: $165-$250

Required for: ICU, ER, PACU, OR, telemetry units

  • Advanced airway management
  • Pharmacology and IV access
  • ECG interpretation
  • Post-cardiac arrest care

Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)

Provider: American Heart Association

Duration: 2 years

Prerequisites: Current BLS certification

Cost: $165-$250

Required for: Pediatric units, PICU, NICU, emergency departments

  • Pediatric assessment and recognition
  • Respiratory and shock management
  • Cardiac arrest algorithms
  • Team dynamics and communication

üìÖ Renewal Timeline

  • 6 Months Before: Check expiration dates, plan renewal schedule
  • 3 Months Before: Register for classes, complete online portions
  • 1 Month Before: Complete skills verification, submit documentation
  • Expired Certification: May require full initial course, not just renewal
  • Grace Period: Some employers allow 30-day grace period

üìö Specialty Training Requirements

Critical Care Training

AACN Essentials: Critical care orientation programs

Duration: 6-12 weeks typically

Content: Hemodynamics, ventilator management, pharmacology

Emergency Department Training

ENA Requirements: Trauma, triage, and emergency protocols

Additional: TNCC (Trauma), ENPC (Pediatric Emergency)

Duration: 8-16 weeks orientation

Operating Room Training

AORN Standards: Perioperative nursing orientation

Duration: 6-12 months typically

Content: Sterile technique, instrumentation, patient positioning
